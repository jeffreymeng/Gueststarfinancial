About business casual

GuestStar Financial was founded in 2010 by a group of people who had worked in the high tech industry in Silicon Valley. We share the same background with many of the engineers and managers working in the Bay Area. We started the company by investing our own money in real estate. The investments have turned out to be very rewarding to us. We want to share our knowledge and experience with you and help you build your financial success as well. We serve all your needs in your home purchase/sale or real estate investments, including financing and refinancing. We are truly the end-to-end solution provider you can count on.
